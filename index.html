OBJECTIVES OF THE CENTRE

  1. To develop testing and selection schemes for further improvement of dairy and economic merit.
  2. To establish Bulk Milk Collection Centre (BMCCs) for milk collection and processing (value addition)
  3. To conduct a diagnostic survey of inventorization and characterization of the Nigerian dairy industry
  4. To advocate and popularize peri-urban dairying concept
  5. To establish Bulk Milk Collection Centre (BMCCs) for milk collection and processing (value addition)
  6. To develop testing and selection schemes for further improvement of dairy and economic merit.

Mission

To conduct demand-driven research into the entire dairy value chain using improved animal genetic resources, well capacitated human resources and good quality material resources through strategic planning to sustainably develop the Nigerian dairy industry in an environmentally smart and friendly fashion.

Vision

To be a world class centre for dairy research and development comparable to any in the world.
