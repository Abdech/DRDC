The research research and development will bring a lot of developments, stability and a sense of belonging to the Nigeria universities and academics in the North-East.

Mission

To conduct demand-driven research into the entire dairy value chain using improved animal genetic resources, well capacitated human resources and good quality material resources through strategic planning to sustainably develop the Nigerian dairy industry in an environmentally smart and friendly fashion.

Vision

To be a world class center for dairy research and development comparable to any in the world.

Mandate

  • To conduct research into all the three Components of dairying (Production, Processing and Marketing/Distribution
  • To conduct research into all areas and Programmes of dairying (Breeding, Reproduction, Feed Production, Feeding, Engineering, Socio-Economics etc)
  • To extend generated technologies to industry (Triple Helix Model) in order to sustainably develop the Nigerian Dairy Industry
  • To train and retrain critical stakeholders of the dairy industry Etc
